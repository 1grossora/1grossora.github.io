Some Science Stuff

My Thesis was done with the MicroBooNE detector at Fermi Nation Accelerator Lab (FNAL) in Batavia, Il. The detector is large Liquid Argon Time Projection Chamber (LArTPC) which is to study the properties of neutrinos, a fundamental particle of nature. I joined the collaboration begining of the construction phase and was lucky to play an integral role assebling the primary system of the detector.
